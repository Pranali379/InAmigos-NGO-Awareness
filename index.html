ABOUT US

Empowering Communities Through Compassion & Action

InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization dedicated to creating positive social change across India. Since 2020, the foundation has been actively working in food distribution, education, women empowerment, environmental protection, animal welfare and youth skill development.

We believe that every individual deserves equal opportunities, dignity and hope. Through our volunteers, interns and supporters, we strive to create sustainable solutions that transform lives.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To improve lives by promoting education, healthcare, food security, environmental sustainability, women empowerment and community development through collective efforts and volunteer participation.

Our Vision

To build an inclusive society where every individual has equal access to opportunities, resources and a better quality of life through compassion, innovation and service.

OUR PROJECTS

Creating Change Through Meaningful Initiatives

Every initiative by InAmigos Foundation focuses on improving lives, promoting equality, and building a better future for communities across India.

Project Seva

Food and clothing distribution drives ensuring no family sleeps hungry.

Project Bachpanshala

Providing education, books and learning opportunities to underprivileged children.

Project Udaan

Empowering women through skill development and livelihood programs.

Project Jeev

Caring for stray animals through rescue, feeding and awareness campaigns.

Project Prakriti

Tree plantation drives and environmental awareness for a greener tomorrow.

Project Vikas

Internship and volunteer programs that inspire youth to create social impact.
